About sgCrease

Hello! I'm sgCrease, a former online multiplayer addict who's on a mission to experience the critically acclaimed single-player games I've missed over the years.

My Gaming Journey

For nearly a decade, I was completely immersed in competitive online multiplayer games. From grinding ranks in CS:GO to chasing wins in Fortnite and League of Legends, my gaming life revolved around competition and online interactions.

In 2023, I decided to break the cycle and explore the narrative-rich, immersive experiences I'd been neglecting. This tier list documents my journey through these acclaimed games.

Why I'm Doing This

I started this project to:

  • Experience storytelling masterpieces I've missed
  • Broaden my gaming horizons beyond competitive titles
  • Share my authentic reactions as a newcomer to these games
  • Create a resource for others in similar situations

Current Challenge

I'm currently working through a list of 50+ critically acclaimed single-player games. My goal is to complete them all and provide honest ratings based on my personal experience.

Final Review & Ranking

My Rating Method

I rate games based on my personal experience as a former multiplayer addict exploring single-player games for the first time. Here's how I evaluate each game:

Rating Categories

Each game receives a score from 0-10 in these categories, which are combined for the final rating:

Story & Narrative (30%)

Quality of storytelling, character development, and emotional impact

Gameplay & Mechanics (30%)

Combat systems, controls, progression, and overall fun factor

World Design (20%)

Environment design, immersion, and exploration satisfaction

Presentation (10%)

Visuals, sound design, music, and technical performance

Personal Impact (10%)

How much the game resonated with me personally

Tier System

Based on the final rating, games are placed in one of five tiers:

  • S Tier (9-10): Masterpiece - Sets a new standard for excellence
  • A Tier (7-8): Excellent - Highly recommended with minor flaws
  • B Tier (5-6): Good - Enjoyable but with noticeable issues
  • C Tier (3-4): Average - Mediocre experience with some redeeming qualities
  • D Tier (0-2): Poor - Significant flaws outweigh any positives

Completion Status

I only rate games I've completed at least the main story for. Games I haven't finished are marked as "In Progress" and won't appear in the tier list until completed.
